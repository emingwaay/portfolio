Specta
Speculative Design
Triennale di Milano
2023
Litia, an high tech corporation, developed Specta: your personal eco-anxiety silencer. Specta is the result of a speculative design project about climate change. It’s a device that protects against eco-anxiety by silencing the screens that show harmful visual materials about climate change and which leads to seeing them from the rock’s perspective. Infact through Specta, it is possible to watch the same events related to climate change from the perspective of the rocks, seen as calming stratifications, and enter into a state of contemplation.
Team: Francesco Bonetti, Enrico Isidori, Federico Gajo, Cecilia Pizzagalli, Camilla Tosi

Pensiero Selvaggio
Art Direction / Branding

2024
The rebranding project for Pensiero Selvaggio takes inspiration from the intricate symbolism and aesthetic heritage of the Dogon people, channeling their cultural depth into a bold and evocative visual identity. 2024
The audiovisual project for “Varese Scrim” enhances the original work’s multisensory experience through innovative design.
